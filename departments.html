Our Medical Departments

Specialized healthcare services delivered with compassion and excellence.

Fitwi Central Hospital provides a full range of specialized departments designed to deliver high-quality healthcare. Each department is staffed with experienced professionals and equipped with modern medical technology to ensure the best patient outcomes.

Internal Medicine

Diagnosis and treatment of adult diseases, chronic conditions, and preventive care.

General Surgery

Advanced surgical procedures performed using modern techniques and equipment.

Gynecology

Women’s health services including reproductive care and pregnancy support.

Pediatrics

Healthcare for infants, children, and adolescents focusing on growth and development.

Cardiology

Heart care including diagnosis, treatment, and cardiovascular disease management.

Emergency Care

24/7 emergency medical services for urgent and life-threatening conditions.

Why Choose Our Departments?

Our departments are designed to provide patient-centered care with advanced technology, experienced specialists, and a commitment to excellence in every treatment.
